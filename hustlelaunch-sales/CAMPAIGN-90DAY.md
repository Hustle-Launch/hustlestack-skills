## Phase 2: Outreach Machine (Days 15-45)

### Week 3-4: Cold Email Sequences

**Sequence 1: Assessment Hook (5 emails over 14 days)**

Email 1 (Day 0) — The Hook:
```
Subject: Found something on {company}'s site

Hi {first_name},

Ran a quick audit on {domain} — found {specific_issue}.

{one_sentence_impact}

Worth 15 min to show you what I found?

— Michael
```

Email 2 (Day 3) — The Proof:
```
Subject: Re: Found something on {company}'s site

{first_name},

Here's what I mean: {screenshot or specific example}

Fixed this for {similar_company} last month — they're up 40% in leads.

Quick call this week?
```

Email 3 (Day 7) — The Breakup Tease:
```
Subject: Should I close your file?

{first_name},

Haven't heard back — guessing timing's off.

I'll keep your Growth Plan on file. When you're ready to {fix_main_issue}, just reply.

— Michael
```

Email 4 (Day 10) — Case Study:
```
Subject: How {similar_company} added $12K/mo

{first_name},

Quick case study: {similar_company} had the same {issue} you do.

3 months later: {result}.

Want me to send the full breakdown?
```

Email 5 (Day 14) — Final Value:
```
Subject: Free Growth Plan for {company}

{first_name},

Put together a custom Growth Plan for {company}.

Shows exactly what's costing you leads and how to fix it.

Want me to send it over? No strings.
```

**Daily Targets:**
- [ ] Send 50 cold emails per day (250/week)
- [ ] Track opens, replies, meetings booked
- [ ] A/B test subject lines weekly

### Week 5-6: Multi-Channel Layering

**LinkedIn Sequence (parallel to email):**
1. Day 0: Connection request (no pitch)
2. Day 2: Thanks for connecting + soft value
3. Day 5: Share relevant content/case study
4. Day 10: Direct pitch if engaged

**Cold Calling Script:**
```
"Hi {first_name}, this is Michael from HustleLaunch.

I help {industry} businesses get more customers from Google.

Took a look at {company}'s site — found a few things that might be 
costing you leads. 

Got 2 minutes to share what I found?"

[If yes] → Share 1-2 specific issues → Book discovery call
[If no] → "No problem. Can I email you the findings?"
```

**Daily Targets:**
- [ ] 20 cold calls per day
- [ ] 30 LinkedIn connections per day
- [ ] Track call outcomes (voicemail, connected, meeting)

### Video Prospecting (High-Value Leads Only)

For leads with $10K+ potential:
- Record 60-90 second Loom
- Screen share their site, show specific issues
- Personal, not scripted
- Send via email with thumbnail

**Target:** 5 video prospecting emails per day

## Phase 3: Conversion Engine (Days 46-75)

### Week 7-8: Discovery Call Process

**Discovery Call Structure (30 min):**

0-5 min: Rapport
- How'd you get into {industry}?
- How long have you been in business?

5-15 min: Pain Discovery
- Where are most of your customers coming from now?
- What happens when someone searches for {service} in {city}?
- How much is a new customer worth to you?
- What have you tried before? What worked/didn't?

15-25 min: Solution Overview
- Walk through Growth Plan findings
- Show competitor comparison
- Explain your approach (not deliverables, outcomes)
- Share relevant case study

25-30 min: Next Steps
- "Based on what you've told me, I'd recommend [tier]"
- "Investment is $X/month with a $Y setup"
- "Want me to send over a proposal?"

**Qualification Criteria (BANT):**
- Budget: Can afford $1,500+/mo
- Authority: Decision maker or can get to one
- Need: Clear pain point we solve
- Timeline: Ready to start within 30 days

### Week 9-10: Proposal & Close Process

**Proposal Template Sections:**
1. Executive Summary (their problem, your solution)
2. Discovery Findings (from call)
3. Recommended Solution (tier + customizations)
4. Investment (monthly + setup + timeline)
5. Case Studies (2-3 relevant)
6. Next Steps (sign + kickoff date)

**Follow-Up Sequence Post-Proposal:**
- Day 1: "Proposal sent — any questions?"
- Day 3: "Checking in — had a chance to review?"
- Day 5: Call (don't email)
- Day 7: "Want to schedule a quick call to walk through it?"
- Day 10: "Should I close this out or is timing just off?"

**Negotiation Guardrails:**
- Never discount more than 15%
- Trade value, don't cut price (add service, extend term)
- Minimum 3-month commitment
- Setup fee is non-negotiable

## Phase 4: Scale & Optimize (Days 76-90)

### Week 11-12: Referral & Partnership Machine

**Client Referral Program:**
- $500 credit for every referral that closes
- Ask for referrals at 30-day mark (after first wins)
- Quarterly "referral bonus" email to all clients

**Partnership Targets:**
- Accountants/bookkeepers (they see financials, know who needs help)
- Business attorneys (new business formations)
- Commercial insurance agents (same client base)
- Complementary agencies (dev shops that don't do marketing)
- Business coaches/consultants

**Partnership Pitch:**
```
"I help your clients get more customers. You look good, I get leads.

For every client you send that closes, I'll send you $500 or a referral back.

Want to grab coffee and see if there's a fit?"
```

**Target:** 3 partnership meetings per week

### Week 13: Automation & Handoff

**Automate:**
- Lead scoring (auto-prioritize by assessment scores + engagement)
- Email sequences (Instantly, Smartlead, or Apollo)
- Meeting scheduling (Calendly with qualification questions)
- CRM updates (Zapier: email reply → update status)
- Proposal generation (template + merge fields)

**Hire/Delegate:**
- SDR for cold calling ($15-20/hr, commission on meetings)
- VA for list building ($5-10/hr)
- Keep closing calls yourself (for now)

## Emergency Plays (If Behind Target)

**Not enough leads?**
- Buy a list (Apollo, ZoomInfo)
- Hire Fiverr VA for scraping
- Run Facebook/Google ads to assessment tool

**Not enough replies?**
- Test new subject lines
- Shorten emails (under 50 words)
- Add video prospecting
- Try different send times

**Not enough closes?**
- Record discovery calls, review for leaks
- Get proposal feedback from lost deals
- Lower barrier (month-to-month vs 3-mo)
- Add urgency (limited spots, price increase)

**Cash flow tight?**
- Offer annual prepay discount (20% off)
- Setup fee upfront, monthly starts after
- Productized quick-win service ($500-1K)
